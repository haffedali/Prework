About the boys

BROCKHAMPTON is a California based boyband fouded by Kevin Abstract. He's got some abstract ideas when it comes to norms of the music industry!

The group met in a Kanye West forum, where Kevin reached out to other skilled rappers. He wanted to make a group, and the boys were all for it. They all had their own careers at this point, but they hadn't taken off yet.
